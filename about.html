About Us

We are Promed Staffing.

A healthcare company with years of recruitment experience.

We supply flexible Healthcare Assistants and Nurses to care homes, nursing homes and hospitals throughout the UK on both a temporary and permanent basis.

We only work with recruiting partners who share our ethics and treat candidates with respect. That means recruitment fit for people and fair fees for all.

We offer hassle-free healthcare recruitment

We know you need reliable, experienced staff who can hit the ground running.So we created a recruitment process that's fair, fast and flexible, and delivers reliable candidates at short notice.

From your first enquiry to hiring healthcare assistants or nurses and getting things back on track: We strictly pre-screen our healthcare workers' experience, qualifications, references, criminal records and English communication skills, so you gain staff who are ready and eager to work

What we stand for at Promed Staffing.

Unique needs, deserve a unique approach!

That is why there's no such thing as one-size-fits-all at Promed Staffing because every case is different. If you're a client that means we listen first before we talk solutions.

If you're a candidate, we promise to be upfront about what you can expect and the kind of work you'll do. We'll also do our best to match your skills, experience and preferences with a role you'll want to stay in.

Matching the right candidate first time

Despite how quick and easy recruiting healthcare workers with Promedstaffing is, we take extra care so you don't have to repeat the process when you need a role quickly.

Our goal is to match the right healthcare worker with the right job first time around, because we realise that long-term relationships in care are best for both our businesses
