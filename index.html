About Us

We are Promed Staffing.

A healthcare company with years of recruitment experience.

We supply flexible Healthcare Assistants and Nurses to care homes, nursing homes and hospitals throughout the UK on both a temporary and permanent basis.

International Recruitment.

Recruiting can prove to be a time consuming and costly endeavour for employers, which is why so many healthcare facilities trust us to help them find healthcare staff when they need them.

That is why finding the right health worker for medical teams is our specialty

Expert training for healthcare workers

Our clients say it's the quality of training that sets us apart from our rivals. It all begins with our mandatory training courses, which covers the crucial knowledge we believe every care assistant should possess, with subjects like safeguarding, infection control and emergency first aid

With a variety of trainings in various fields, we train our staff to give their best.

Permanent Placements

Staffing cover when you need it most. We understand the urgency of finding healthcare workers at short notice, so we provide a service that is suitable to cover most time frames.

All our healthcare workers are trained to integrate seamlessly with your own team of staff.
